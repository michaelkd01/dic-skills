Essential Repairs
Immediate safety and repair items we recommend addressing first.
Essential Repairs Home Stewardship Plan
Roof drainagePriority  ·  By quotation
Clear and treat the surface corrosion in the box gutter and restore free drainage.
Why it matters   Free-flowing roof drainage protects the roof structure and prevents water entry during heavy rain.
Deck balustradePriority  ·  By quotation
Re-fix the movement at the upper-deck balustrade posts to restore full rigidity.
Why it matters   A firm balustrade is an essential safeguard against falls.
Pool barrier compliancePriority  ·  By quotation
Adjust the self-closing pool gate and renew the faded safety signage so the barrier meets compliance.
Why it matters   A compliant pool barrier is a legal requirement and a vital safeguard for young children.
Subfloor timberPriority  ·  By quotation
Address the elevated moisture at the subfloor bearer and treat the affected timber.
Why it matters   Managing subfloor moisture protects the structural timbers that support your home.
Electrical safetyPriority  ·  By quotation
Install RCD protection to the unprotected circuits and complete safety-switch testing across the board.
Why it matters   Modern safety switches protect your household from electric shock.
Smoke-alarm compliancePriority  ·  By quotation
Upgrade to interconnected photoelectric smoke alarms and confirm correct placement and coverage throughout the home.
Why it matters   Interconnected alarms give your household the earliest possible warning in the event of a fire.
